Who We Are

Feeding communities,
building futures

Feeds Kenya is a charitable agricultural initiative dedicated to improving livestock nutrition and farmer livelihoods across Kenya and the broader East African region.

We believe that well-nourished animals are the cornerstone of a thriving farming household. Through targeted product distribution, expert consultations, and community events, we work hand-in-hand with farmers to build sustainable agricultural practices.

What We Offer

Everything your farm needs

From quality feeds to expert guidance, we provide comprehensive support for farmers at every stage.

Quality Animal Feeds

Nutritionally balanced feeds for cattle, goats, sheep, poultry, and more — sourced from certified suppliers.

Nutritional Consultation

One-on-one sessions with our agricultural experts to develop custom feeding programmes for your livestock.

Farm Events & Workshops

Regular training sessions, exhibitions, and knowledge-sharing events to sharpen your farming skills.

Livestock Health Clinics

Mobile health clinics bringing veterinary care and preventative treatments directly to your farm.

Subsidy Programmes

Government and NGO-backed subsidy schemes that reduce the cost of premium agricultural inputs for smallholder farmers.

Farmer Community

Connect with thousands of farmers sharing knowledge, experiences, and best practices across the region.
